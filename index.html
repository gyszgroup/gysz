About GYSZ Group

Building bridges across industries with a diverse portfolio spanning agriculture, environmental solutions, sports, hospitality, manufacturing, and professional services that create lasting value for communities worldwide.

GYSZ Group represents a dynamic conglomerate with deep roots in essential industries that serve communities worldwide. From our origins in farming, transportation, and mining in India through London Dollar Predecessor, we have evolved into a diversified business empire spanning agriculture, environmental technology, sports, hospitality, manufacturing, and professional services.

Our portfolio encompasses everything from sustainable farming and waste management to premium hospitality experiences and specialized manufacturing. We believe in building businesses that not only generate profits but also contribute meaningfully to society - whether through environmental conservation, sports charity initiatives, or providing essential services to ports, restaurants, and pet owners.

With operations across India, the Middle East, and the USA, GYSZ Group maintains a commitment to excellence in every sector we serve. Our companies work synergistically, sharing expertise and resources while maintaining their unique identities and specialized focus areas, creating a robust ecosystem of interconnected businesses.
